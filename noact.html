Nydegger Online Automatic Cognitive Test

The NOACT is a timed, computerized IQ assessment consisting of 50 items, designed to measure cognitive abilities ranging from one to five standard deviations above the average.

The test covers numerical, verbal, logical (logico-divergent) and abstract reasoning, with items roughly arranged in order of increasing difficulty.

While similar in format to the NAIT, the NOACT differs by including a time limit of 360 hours (15 days), making it a hybrid between a standard high-range IQ test and a supervised assessment.

Note: The NOACT is an online application that performs best on desktop computer browsers.
