Nydegger Online Automatic Cognitive Test

The NOACT is a computerized high-range cognitive assessment consisting of 50 items covering numerical, verbal, logical, and abstract reasoning.

Items are presented in approximate order of increasing difficulty and are designed to assess abilities approximately from one to five standard deviations above the average.

While the format is similar to the NAIT, the NOACT has a time limit of 360 hours (15 days).

This limit exists solely for practical reasons. The test is not speed-based, the time spent solving items has no influence on the score.

Note: The NOACT is an online application that performs best on modern desktop computer browsers.

Participation is usually free of charge. Please do not share or discuss the test items with others, AI tools are not allowed for solving, translating, analyzing or checking answers.

You may use dictionaries and resources such as Wikipedia.
