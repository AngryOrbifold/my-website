Nydegger Extreme Automatic Intelligence Test Online

The NEAITO is an online, untimed and computerized IQ assessment containing 60 items.

It is a revised version of the NAIT-ES: some items have been updated, 10 easier items have been added, and the test is now fully accessible online.

These items cover numerical, verbal, logical, abstract, and spatial reasoning and are approximately arranged in order of increasing difficulty.

While similar in design to the NAIT, the NEAITO features entirely new items and can be seen as a continuation.

This test is very challenging and designed to assess IQ scores higher than those measurable by most other tests.

It is recommend that new candidates take other tests before attempting the NEAITO.

Note: For candidates who have already started the NAIT-ES, your progress has been carried over to the NEAITO, and any certificates remain valid.

Participation is usually free of charge. Please do not share or discuss test items with others. The use of AI tools for solving, analyzing, or checking answers is not permitted.

You may use (online) calculators and dictionaries.
