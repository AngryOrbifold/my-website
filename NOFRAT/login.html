Nydegger Online Formal Reasoning Automatic Test

Instructions:
NOFRAT is a timed, high-range cognitive test.
You have 480 hours to work on 55 items. A timer will display how much time remains.
Items are presented in roughly increasing order of difficulty, but you may answer them in any order you choose.
Throughout the test, you have 35 attempts. Each incorrect answer costs one attempt.
The test ends when either the timer reaches 0, your remaining attempts reach 0 or all items have been solved.
Answers are not case-sensitive.
For spatial items, you will be provided with a canvas on which you must draw your answer.
All spatial answers are matrices composed of black and white cells. When drawing, you must choose the matrix dimensions and the color of each cell.
You will answer each item as it appears, there is no option to submit all answers at once.

Important:
Once you click "Start Test", the countdown begins.
Your time and attempts will continue to run even if you close your browser.
So please make sure you are properly prepared and motivated to finish before starting.
