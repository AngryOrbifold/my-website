Nydegger Online Automatic Intelligence Scale

NOAIS is an untimed, high-range intelligence test consisting of 88 items, designed to measure cognitive ability from one standard deviation below the mean up to six standard deviations above the mean.

The test includes items assessing numerical, verbal, abstract, and spatial reasoning, arranged approximately in order of increasing difficulty.

NOAIS comprises two independent (sub)tests: NOFRAT and OVAT33.

NOFRAT evaluates cognitive ability using non-verbal items and is suitable for candidates who are not proficient in English or who prefer a culture-fair assessment.

OVAT33 is the verbal subtest of NOAIS and is not considered a standalone intelligence test.

Although NOFRAT alone is regarded as a full intelligence test, it is recommended to take the verbal subtest as well whenever possible.
