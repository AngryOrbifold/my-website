Nydegger Automatic Intelligence Test

The NAIT is an untimed, self-scored IQ assessment consisting of 120 items designed to measure cognitive ability beyond the range of standard IQ tests.

The items—spanning numerical, verbal, logical, abstract, and spatial reasoning—are approximately arranged in order of increasing difficulty.

While not a substitute for professionally administered psychological evaluations, the NAIT aims to serve as a complementary tool for cognitive measurement.

Participation is free of charge. Please do not discuss the test items with others under any circumstances.
