Nydegger Intelligence Tests

On this website, you will find experimental intelligence tests that I have created or co-authored.

This project began over a decade ago with the NIT-Form 1, but was discontinued around 2015.

It has now been revived in a new format with improved items. All tests here are self-scoring computer programs, unlike most high-range IQ tests that are scored manually.

This enables candidates to receive instant feedback, potentially making the testing experience more engaging and enjoyable for some.

Although my background is in mechanics and computer science rather than psychometrics, I place the highest importance on ensuring these tests are as valid and accurate as possible. They will therefore continue to be refined and updated over time as new data become available.

All My Tests

NAIT

A 120-item, self-scored test covering numerical, verbal, logical, abstract, and spatial reasoning. Quite long, but offers a very wide IQ measurement range.

NAIT-ES

A 50-item, self-scored test covering the same domains as NAIT, but shorter, harder, and with an increased ceiling for higher IQ measurement.

SAT36

A 36-item, high-range spatial IQ test, co-created with Andrei Udriște.
