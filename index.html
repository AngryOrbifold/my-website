Computerized high range cognitive tests

On this website, you will find experimental intelligence tests that I have created or co-authored.

This project began in 2009 with MEIT63, was enhanced and launched online in 2012 as NIT-Form 1, but was discontinued around 2015. It has now been revived in a new format, featuring improved items and updated scoring methods.

The tests here are designed to be more challenging than standard IQ assessments. With a higher ceiling, they are designed to measure exceptional cognitive abilities that conventional tests cannot capture.

All tests are untimed, allowing candidates to focus fully on reasoning and problem solving without time pressure.

This power test format ensures that results reflect true ability rather than speed, and are less influenced by factors such as age or state of health.

Unlike most high range IQ tests, which are scored manually, every test on this site uses automated scoring, providing instant and objective feedback.

Whether you are seeking a challenge, a hobby, or just want to measure your IQ, you can take all tests on this website at no cost.

Standalone tests

SAITO

A 30-item, untimed, online computerized test assessing numerical, verbal, logical, abstract, and spatial reasoning. Designed as a brief introduction and demonstration, this test is recommended as a starting point for those new to high range tests.
Overall Test Complexity: medium

NAIT

A 120-item, untimed, computerized test covering numerical, verbal, logical, abstract, and spatial reasoning. Quite long, but offers a wide IQ measurement range.
Overall Test Complexity: medium

NEAITO

A 60-item, untimed, online computerized test covering the same domains as NAIT, but shorter, harder, and with an increased ceiling for higher IQ measurement.
Overall Test Complexity: very high

NOACT

A 50-item, untimed, online computerized test covering numerical, verbal, logical, and abstract reasoning with progressive difficulty.
Overall Test Complexity: high

NOFRAT

A 55-item, untimed, online computerized culture-fair test assessing numerical, abstract, and spatial reasoning with progressive difficulty.
Overall Test Complexity: very high

OVAT 33

A 33-item, untimed, online computerized, high range verbal subtest of NOAIS.
Overall Test Complexity: medium

SAT 36

A 36-item, high-range spatial test, co-created with Andrei Udriște.
Overall Test Complexity: high

Combined tests

NOAIS

A 88-item, untimed, online computerized high range test covering numerical, verbal, abstract and spatial reasoning with progressive difficulty. It is the fusion of NOFRAT and OVAT33.
Overall Test Complexity: high
