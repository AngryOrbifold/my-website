Nydegger Automatic Intelligence Test

The NAIT is an untimed, self-scored IQ test consisting of 122 items of varying difficulty, designed to measure intelligence beyond the range of common IQ tests like the WAIS.
The items—numerical, verbal, logical, and spatial—are roughly arranged in order of increasing difficulty.
