Nydegger Automatic Intelligence Test

The NAIT is an untimed, computerized, self-scoring IQ assessment consisting of 120 items designed to measure cognitive ability beyond the range of standard IQ tests.

The items cover numerical, verbal, logical, abstract, and spatial reasoning, and are approximately arranged in order of increasing difficulty.

While not a replacement for professionally administered psychological evaluations, the NAIT is intended as a complementary tool for measuring cognitive ability.

Note: The NAIT is a standalone computer program that runs only on Windows. (~900 Mb compressed)

Participation is free of charge. Do not share and discuss the test items with others or use any form of AI.

To receive the test, please authenticate yourself by sending a photo of your ID card or passport to this Email address.
