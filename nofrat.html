Nydegger Online Formal Reasoning Automatic Test

NOFRAT is a computerized, high-range cognitive assessment consisting of 55 items spanning numerical, abstract, and spatial reasoning.

It is designed as a culture-fair alternative to NAIT and NAIT-ES, requiring only minimal prior knowledge to solve.

Items are presented in an approximate order of increasing difficulty and are intended to assess abilities ranging from about one to six standard deviations above the average.

The NOFRAT has a total time limit of 480 hours (20 days).

This limit exists solely for practical reasons. The test is not speed-based, and the amount of time spent solving items has no influence on the final score.

Note: NOFRAT is an online application that performs best on modern desktop browsers.

Participation is usually free of charge. Please do not share or discuss test items with others. The use of AI tools for solving, analyzing, or checking answers is not permitted and will not be helpful.
