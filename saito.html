Short Automatic Intelligence Test Online

SAITO is an untimed, computerized, high range Intelligence test consisting of 30 items covering numerical, abstract, verbal, logical, and spatial reasoning.

SAITO is a demonstration and practice test designed for individuals who are unfamiliar with high range tests or who are unsure whether to authenticate and take the other tests available on this website.

Candidates need not be authenticated to take SAITO, verifying an email address is sufficient.

SAITO follows the same format as NOACT and NOAIS, but in a shorter, condensed version.

Note: This test is an online application that performs well on smartphones, tablets, and desktop browsers.

Participation is free of charge. Please do not share or discuss test items with others. The use of AI tools to solve, analyze, or check answers is not permitted.

Please do not retake the test using another email address, as this defeats the purpose of the assessment.

You may use calculators and dictionaries, including online versions.
