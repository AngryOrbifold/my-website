Online Verbal Automatic Test 33

OVAT 33 is a computerized, untimed, high-range verbal cognitive test comprising 33 items.

It serves as the verbal subtest of the Nydegger Online Automatic Intelligence Scale (NOAIS), which also includes the NOFRAT for the non-verbal part.

The items are arranged in an approximate order of increasing difficulty and are designed to measure verbal abilities spanning from roughly one standard deviation below the mean to five standard deviations above.

Participation is usually free of charge. Please do not share or discuss test items with others. The use of AI tools for solving, analyzing, or checking answers is not permitted.

You may use dictionaries and resources such as Wikipedia.
