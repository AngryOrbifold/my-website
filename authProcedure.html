Authentication Procedure

To verify your identity, please follow the steps below:

All new users must submit a valid identity document, either through the test applications or by sending it directly to this email address.

Your full name and date of birth must be clearly visible on the document.

If you are a member of the Glia Society, this information is sufficient. If you are not a member, the document must also include a visible photograph of your face.

In addition, you must submit a recent photograph of yourself. This is required to confirm that the person submitting the information is the same individual named on the identity document.
