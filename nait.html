Nydegger Automatic Intelligence Test

The NAIT is an untimed and computerized IQ assessment consisting of 120 items designed to measure cognitive ability beyond the range of standard IQ tests.

These items cover numerical, verbal, logical, abstract, and spatial reasoning and are approximately arranged in order of increasing difficulty.

While not a replacement for professionally administered psychological evaluations, the NAIT is intended as a complementary tool for measuring cognitive ability.

Note: The NAIT is a standalone computer program that runs only on Windows 10 & 11. (Approx. 780 MB compressed)

Participation is free of charge. Please do not share or discuss the test items with others, nor use any form of AI assistance. You may use dictionaries.

To download the test use one of the links below, know that you will need to authenticate yourself by sending a photo of your ID card or passport through the app.

Your name and date of birth must be visible; you can mask the picture.
