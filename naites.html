Nydegger Automatic Intelligence Test - Extended Scale

The NAIT-ES is an untimed, computerized, self-scoring IQ assessment containing 50 items.

These items cover numerical, verbal, logical, abstract, and spatial reasoning and are approximately arranged in order of increasing difficulty.

It is similar in design to the NAIT but contains entirely new items; it is not a revision of the original test.

The NAIT-ES is designed so that its entry level begins where the ceilings of professionally administered psychological tests lie, allowing it to measure IQ scores higher than those obtainable on commonly used tests.

Note: The NAIT-ES is a standalone program compatible only with Windows 10 and 11. (Approx. 780 MB compressed)

Participation is free of charge. Please do not share or discuss the test items with others, nor use any form of AI assistance. You may use dictionaries and other non-“intelligent” resources.
